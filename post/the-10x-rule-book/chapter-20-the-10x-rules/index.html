Chapter 20 : Omnipresence

  • omnipresence : concept of being everywhere - in all places, at all times
  • accessible everywhere
  • make yourself available everywhere
  • your name is your most important asset. people can take everything away from you but they can’t take your name
  • unless people know who you are, no one will pay attention to what you represent
  • you have to get people to know you which means that you have to get attention
  • the higher your purpose, the more fuel it will provide for your 10X actions
  • once you match your mindset with the right purpose, you will start taking 10X actions
